Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text strings that the sites visited by the user send to its terminal (usually browser) mobile or desktop, where they are stored before being re-transmitted to the same sites at the next visit by the same user, as specified by point 1 of the premise to the regulation n.229 / 2014. For this reason, cookies cannot be considered viruses or malicious programs.

The function of cookies

Through these small text strings it is possible to compare new and past site visitors and understand, therefore, what the users' preferences are.

Cookies and visitors

With this information, in compliance with the provision n. 229/2014, the user is informed that by using the site he allows the entry of cookies as specified above, and that they are installed on his device, thus allowing access.

Third-party cookies

The site allows access to the server to "third party" services, ie allows the entry of cookies that are sent from different sites or web-servers (commonly called "third parties"), on which some elements may reside such as images, maps, sounds, specific links to pages from other domains. The "third parties" use this method to send announcements in the browser of the site user by detecting his IP address and thus allowing access to the aforementioned elements as well as advertising based on the interests of the user.

In line with the provisions of the aforementioned provision it is necessary to inform the user that this site does not have control over the cookies that are used by the "third parties".

Frequently, therefore, it is possible to disable cookies directly from your browser as better specified in the section on "how to control cookies". The user browsing the site, consents the entry of cookies relating to the same site, and consequently, accepts the entry of third-party cookies, except the refusal of consent to this entry through the methods specified in the paragraph "how to control cookies ".
